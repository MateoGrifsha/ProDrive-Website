FAQ

Find the answers for the most frequently asked questions below.

How can I use ProDrive?

Download ProDrive from the section below. This will start the process of downloading the ProDrive Launcher. From there you can create an account or sign in into an existing one. After the sign in process you can play by pressing the Play button.

Why do I need the ProDrive Launcher?

ProDrive Launcher is a useful tool for keeping track of your progress. By having an accessible account you can collect and save newer and better cars.

Can my device support ProDrive?

Currently, we only offer Windows support. In the near future Android support will be added so the user can keep the progress across different devices.

Can my windows device handle ProDrive?

ProDrive comes with 3 graphic options installed. Select different modes varying on the specifications of your device.

Is ProDrive free to use?

Yes. ProDrive is free of cost and free of ads :)

Does the game have constant updates?

Of course! As the game is still in development the updates are vital to the gameplay and offer the user a better overall experience.
